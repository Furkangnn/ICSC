Competition Process

1

Qualification Round

Five problems covering foundational concepts. Minimum scores: Junior (15 pts), Youth (17 pts), Senior (20 pts).

2

Pre-Final Round

Six complex problems over four days. Minimum scores: Junior (16 pts), Youth (20 pts), Senior (24 pts).

3

Final Round

20 multiple-choice questions with 60 seconds each. Fast-paced online exam determining final rankings.
