ICSC 2025 Information

What is ICSC?

The ICSC is a global educational program that combines computer science with advanced mathematics. Students challenge their logical thinking through algorithmic problem-solving, mathematical proofs, and computational complexity theory. The competition bridges the gap between pure mathematics and practical computer science applications.

Prizes and Awards

Showcase your mathematical and computational abilities and compete for cash prizes, certificates, and global recognition. The prizes are worth upwards of 1000 USD for outstanding performance in mathematical problem-solving and algorithmic thinking.

  • 1st, 2nd, 3rd Cash Prizes for each age category
  • National Awards for top performers from each country
  • Bronze, Silver, and Gold Honours for Finalists
  • Special Honours for outstanding submissions
  • Official Participation Certificates for all participants

Competition Rounds

Qualification Round

Five problems covering foundational concepts, logic, and introductory algorithms. Free participation for all students.

Pre-Final Round

Six more complex problems over four days. Registration cost: 11 EUR. Financial aid available.

Final Round

Fast-paced online exam with 20 multiple-choice questions. Free participation for qualified students.
